Medical CRM

If you are interested in how Mobeserv can help you, please get in touch.

Sales force in medical field are looking at newer ways to improve efficiency and enhance productivity so as to face new challenges and seize new opportunities. Mobile technology could disrupt , transform and improve the health care industry with app that connects conversations, devices, processes, services in a whole new way.

The App is designed specifically for field medical teams, empowering them access to relevant and meaning full data to foster meaningful information with a complete view of each client. The App also ensures that each group maintains complete control over shared information, you are always assured of compliance.

Features

For Pharma Companies

  • Allocate and monitor appointment book
  • Geo track their team and track the route followed
  • Order management and inventory details
  • Entire expense tracking
  • Finance department can cross verify the same with the route travelled report
  • Highly flexible analytical reports
  • App is device agnostic and can be made available for all mobile devices

For Medical Reps

  • Manage Appointments with clients
  • Record client interactions
  • View details of customers and reach out to their location through google maps
  • Create client types and manage the same
  • Entire expense tracking
  • App is device agnostic and can be made available for all mobile devices

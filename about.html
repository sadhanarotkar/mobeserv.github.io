About Mobeserv

Finatics Technology Private Limited is a 10 year company having experience in mobile, web apps and product engineering. We work with startups, SMEs and large enterprises. Finatics Technology is a 40+ member team with development centre in Mumbai, India.

Mobeserv is focused on helping businesses to grow by providing relevant enterprise apps in the areas of productivity, customer satisfication, innovation and profitability. Embracing enterprise mobility is a critical requirement for today’s business. By enabling workers to securely access corporate information at any time, from anywhere, your organisation can benefit from increased employee productivity, responsiveness, and accelerated decision-making.
